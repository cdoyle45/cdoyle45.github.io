About me

Casey Doyle

Software developer currently located in Toronto, Canada

Hi! I'm Casey, a software developer and chicken nugget connoisseur currently working in Toronto. I am creative, hard working, and have a passion for creating and learning new technologies.

Ever since I was in grade 8, I knew I wanted to be a software engineer. It was my goal; my dream...and in 2016 my dream was realized and I graduated from Western University with a degree in Software Engineering. While in school, I spent my time participating in a number of hackathons, as well as helping run one at Western. I was also a mentor for Women in Engineering in the Big Sister Little Sister program and an executive for the Women in Technology society.

I currently spend my free time volunteering with the Humane Society as a dog walker, napping, and playing games.
